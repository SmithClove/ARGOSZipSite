About ARGOS

ARGOS is pioneering the future of freight with the first retrofit-ready smart trailer. Designed for the Class 8 dry van segment, our platform integrates electric axle propulsion, real-time telematics, and intelligent compliance systems — all in a drop-in upgrade.

With fuel costs, maintenance, and safety regulations rising, ARGOS helps carriers unlock operational savings, increase uptime, and stay ahead of 2027 EPA standards — without replacing their fleet.

We're building a better trailer — because the future of freight shouldn’t be stuck in the past.
